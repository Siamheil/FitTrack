FITNESS TIPS
Science-backed advice to maximise your results
💧
Hydration is Key

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. During intense sessions, consume 500–750ml per hour to prevent dehydration and keep performance high.

😴
Sleep for Recovery

Aim for 7–9 hours of quality sleep. Growth hormone is released during deep sleep — essential for muscle repair and fat metabolism.

🥩
Protein Timing

Consume 20–40g of protein within 30 minutes post-workout to maximise muscle protein synthesis and speed up recovery.

📈
Progressive Overload

Increase workout intensity by 5–10% each week. This is the most proven method for continuous strength and fitness improvements.

🏃
Warm Up Properly

Spend 5–10 minutes on dynamic stretching before every workout. This improves performance by up to 20% and cuts injury risk significantly.

🍎
Pre-Workout Fuel

Eat complex carbs + protein 1–2 hours before training. Oats with banana, or rice with chicken are ideal pre-workout options.

🔄
Rest Days Matter

Plan 1–2 rest days per week. Muscles grow during recovery, not training. Active recovery like walking or yoga works perfectly on off-days.

🎵
Music Boosts Output

Listening to upbeat music at 120–140 BPM can increase workout endurance by up to 15% and significantly reduce perceived exertion.
